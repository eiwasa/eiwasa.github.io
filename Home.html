About Me

Welcome! ​I’m a senior at Harvey Mudd College studying Engineering with a emphasis in Electrical Engineering. Thanks to Harvey Mudd's general engineering program, I have experience in not just electrical engineering, but also fields such as mechanical, materials, and systems engineering!

Recent Projects

MEMS-ical Devices

Designing a set of comb drive resonators that resonate at the frequencies of specific musical notes and can be tuned using an axial gap closing actuator. Following SOIMUMPS guidelines, my team created the masks needed for fabrication on KLayout.

FastSLAM Algorithm for MR. CLAM

Coded and implemented a FastSLAM 1.0 algorithm to track a robot’s location in an unknown environment while simultaneously mapping surrounding landmarks 

Go to project site

JPL PRA Database Development

I developed and implemented a Probabilistic Risk Assessment (PRA) database for the JPL Systems Reliability Group (5138) to facilitate flight project risk analysis. The database development project focused on the consolidation and entry of PRA data obtained from previous JPL flight missions into a database. Existing flight project reliability data was reviewed, standardized, and used as a basis for the database’s design. The original data was entered into a database with search, compare, and modification capabilities. This new database now supports current PRA endeavors.

Operational Amplifier Design

Designed, constructed, tested, and characterized a dual-supply operational amplifier using BJTs.

Laser Range Finder for Space Applications

I worked on the Millennium Space Systems HMC Clinic Team to devise an opto-mechanical structure that can faciliate a Laser Range Finder device and operations on-orbit. As part of the optics subteam, I developed tests for assessing optical system efficiency, beam width and alignment, and collimation. 

Autonomous Underwater Vehicle

Worked in a team of 4 to create an autonomous underwater vehicle that analyzed water temperature and turbidity with respect to depth, acting as my team's electronics lead. As electronics lead, I designed all of our circuits. This process required inintegrating sensor components of interest, such as thermistors, pressure sensors, photodiodes, and timers.
